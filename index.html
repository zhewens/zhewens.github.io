My review of 2001 - A Space Odyssey in 2014

2015-08-16
Movie Review

“Whoever fights monsters should see to it that in the process he does no become a monster; and if you gaze long enough into an abyss, the abyss will gaze back into you.” By Nietzsche.

What we called 2001: A Space Odyssey today is an epic science fiction film. However, the case was not the same in the year of 1968 when the film screened for the first time. Audiences slept during the show. Even dozens of years later, many people still can’t appreciate it. I myself fall slept twice when I watched the film for the first time. Someone once complained to me that why this film was highly praised if it was so hard to understand. I could not answer her but to quote co-writer of screenplay Arthur C. Clarke’s words that the film was not meant to be understood but to raise questions that far beyond our imagination. I truly agree with it after I have watched it for fourteen times.
